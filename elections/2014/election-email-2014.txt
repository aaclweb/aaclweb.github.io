Additional nominations:

Additional nominations can be submitted until October 31st, 2013.
To make a nomination, three or more NAACL members should send email
to Anoop Sarkar (anoop __at__ sfu.ca), the NAACL secretary, expressing
support for the nominee and giving evidence that he or she will
serve if elected.  (It is recommended that this evidence consist
of a forwarded email from the nominee containing a candidate
statement, a biographical sketch, homepage URL, and a brief affirmation
of intent to serve if elected.) In addition, the nominees for Chair
should meet the criteria set out in the NAACL constitution.

Election process:

The voting period will begin once the final slate of candidates and
voting instructions are announced (soon after October 31st, 2013),
and is scheduled to close on December 15th, 2013.

Many thanks to the Nominating Committee for doing the hard work of
putting together an excellent slate of nominees.

If you were a member of the NAACL last year or the year before and

for 2013 and you will also receive instructions to vote in this
